Refund Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Overview

This Refund Policy applies to all paid subscriptions purchased directly through Sigbot (sigbot.ca) and processed via our payment provider, Paddle. It is governed by the laws of England & Wales and your statutory rights under the Consumer Rights Act 2015 and UK Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 are not affected by this policy.

If you purchased Sigbot through a third-party app store (Apple App Store, Google Play, or Microsoft Store), the refund policy of that store applies and you must request a refund directly from them.

2. 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on first-time purchases of the Pro and Team plans. If you are not satisfied for any reason, contact us within 14 days of your initial payment and we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

To be eligible:

  • The request must be made within 14 calendar days of the first charge.
  • The guarantee applies to first-time subscribers only and does not apply to renewals.
  • One refund per customer account.

3. Subscription Renewals

Sigbot subscriptions renew automatically at the end of each billing period (monthly or annual). We send a reminder email before any annual renewal. Refunds are not issued for renewal charges except in the following circumstances:

  • You did not receive reasonable advance notice of a price change.
  • A billing error occurred on our part (e.g. a duplicate charge).
  • A technical fault on our side prevented you from accessing the Service for a significant portion of the billing period.

If any of the above apply, contact us within 14 days of the charge and we will review your case.

4. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your account billing portal. On cancellation:

  • Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period.
  • You will not be charged again after cancellation.
  • No partial refund is issued for unused time remaining in the current period, except where required by law.

To cancel, log in at app.sigbot.ca, go to Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription, or email us at support@sigbot.co.

5. Annual Plans

Annual subscriptions are billed upfront for a full year. If you cancel an annual plan:

  • Within the 14-day money-back guarantee window — a full refund is issued.
  • After 14 days — no refund is issued for the remaining months, except where required by applicable law.

We recommend using the monthly plan if you are unsure about committing to a full year.

6. Downgrading Plans

If you downgrade from Team to Pro, or from Pro to Starter, the change takes effect at the end of your current billing period. No refund is issued for the difference in plan cost for the current period.

7. Exceptional Circumstances

We handle each refund request fairly and on its own merits. If you experience a significant service outage, a billing error, or another exceptional circumstance not covered above, please contact us and we will work with you to find a fair resolution.

8. How to Request a Refund

Email support@sigbot.co with:

  • Your registered email address
  • The date of the charge
  • The reason for your request

We aim to respond within 2 business days. Approved refunds are processed through Paddle and typically appear on your statement within 5–10 business days depending on your bank or card issuer.

9. Statutory Rights

Nothing in this policy limits or excludes your statutory rights as a consumer under UK or EU law. Where you have a legal right to a refund that is broader than this policy, that right takes precedence.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. The current version is always published at sigbot.ca/refund.html. Material changes will be communicated by email to active subscribers.
